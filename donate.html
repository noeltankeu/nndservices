DONATE

Through our donation service, NND SERVICES connects people who want to give away gently used furniture or other items with those in need. We collect your donated items and ensure they are delivered to families or organizations that can make good use of them. This service not only helps the environment by reducing waste but also supports community members in need.

  1. Acceptance of Donations

    2. NND SERVICES reserves the right to accept or decline any donated items based on their condition, suitability, and the needs of the recipients. Items must be in gently used or good condition, free from significant damage, stains, or excessive wear. The final decision regarding the acceptability of any donated item will be made by NND SERVICES. If an item is deemed unsuitable for donation, NND SERVICES may refuse to collect the item or dispose of it responsibly.

  2. Pick-up Fees

    3. Donors are required to cover the cost of pick-up services. The fee for pick-up will be communicated in advance based on the location, size, and quantity of the items. Payment for the pick-up service must be made prior to or at the time of collection.

  3. Scheduling and Cancellations

    4. Pick-up appointments must be scheduled in advance and are subject to availability. Donors will be provided with a date and time window for the collection of their items. If a donor needs to cancel or reschedule a pick-up, they must notify NND SERVICES at least 24 hours in advance. Failure to do so may result in forfeiture of any fees paid.

  4. Condition of Items

    5. Donors are required to provide accurate descriptions and, if possible, photos of the items they wish to donate. NND SERVICES relies on this information to assess the suitability of items before pick-up. Items that are heavily damaged, broken, or in need of significant repair may not be accepted. NND SERVICES will make the final determination during pick-up and may refuse any item that does not meet quality standards.

  5. Ownership and Transfer of Items

    6. By donating items to NND SERVICES, the donor relinquishes all ownership rights to the items. Once collected, the items become the property of NND SERVICES and may be distributed to those in need, recycled, or disposed of at the company’s discretion.

  6. Liability

    7. NND SERVICES is not responsible for any loss or damage to items once they have been collected. Donors should ensure that all items are appropriately packed and ready for transport. NND SERVICES will take reasonable care in handling items during pick-up, but the company is not liable for any damage that may occur during the transportation of donated goods.

  7. Privacy and Data Protection

    8. NND SERVICES respects the privacy of all donors and will not share personal information with third parties without consent, except as necessary to facilitate the donation process. Donor information will be stored securely and used only for purposes related to the donation service.

  8. Agreement

    9. By scheduling a donation pick-up with NND SERVICES, donors acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to these terms and conditions.
